========================================
  DayZ ESP - Ready to Use!
========================================

EVERYTHING IS INTEGRATED AND READY!

How to Compile:
1. Open "DayZ Steam.sln" in Visual Studio
2. Select "Release" and "x64"
3. Press F7 to compile
4. Executable will be in: x64\Release\Steam.exe

How to Use:
1. Start DayZ and join a server
2. Run Steam.exe as Administrator
3. Press INSERT to open menu
4. Configure your settings
5. Press INSERT again to close menu

Menu Features:
- Beautiful particle system (80 particles)
- Smooth animations
- Modern design with gradients
- 6 tabs: ESP, Aimbot, Loot, Misc, Stats, Config
- Save/Load configurations
- Real-time FPS counter

Controls:
- INSERT: Toggle menu on/off
- Mouse: Navigate and configure
- ESC: Close menu

Everything is connected and working!
Just compile and enjoy!
